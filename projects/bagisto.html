The Challenge

The legacy interface was cluttered and difficult for non-technical merchants to manage. Mobile conversion rates were low due to non-responsive checkout flows and slow rendering on lower-end devices.

The Solution

Implemented a robust design system with consistent atomic components. Redesigned the checkout process into a streamlined 3-step accordion, and optimized asset delivery for lightning-fast speeds.
